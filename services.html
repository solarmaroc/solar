Services

Solar Maroc is a full-service design-build solar solutions company employing integrated design principles to build the most efficient and cost effective PV systems. We are optimized to work with customers during the renewable energy planning and overall project design stage, providing what we call value engineering with the goal of creating state-of-the-art designs to reduce environmental impacts of materials, and enhance indoor environmental quality. Some of the most innovative design engineers rely on the consulting services of Solar Maroc to design efficient photovoltaic (PV) grid-tied and off-grid electricity generation systems. Large or small, Solar Maroc designs and builds custom solar solutions using only proven components from the most reputable manufacturers who work with our solar installers to improve designs for ease of installation, reliability, and value. Included in our services are consulting for solar design and solar installations, certified Hydrost op installations and indoor environmental controls and monitors.
